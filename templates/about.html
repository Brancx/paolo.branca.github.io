About L'Archetto

We are located in a charming late 19th-century building, just a 2-minute walk from Ottaviano/S.Pietro Metro Station on Line A, right in the heart of Rome, near iconic landmarks such as the Vatican Museums and St. Peter's Basilica.

Our prime location, within walking distance of the Vatican Museums, Sistine Chapel, St. Peter's Basilica, Castel Sant'Angelo, and numerous other historical and archaeological sites, ensures convenience and easy accessibility:

  • 1-minute walk from Ottaviano/S.Pietro Metro Station on Line A
  • 2 metro stops from Piazza del Popolo and Via del Corso (15 mins walk)
  • 3 metro stops from Piazza di Spagna and Trevi Fountain (20 mins walk)
  • 6 metro stops from Termini Station
